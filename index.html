ENERGY-BOOSTING TREATMENTS

Mind, Body, & Soul.

MEDITATION

Meditation brings you into a deeper state of awareness. The healing vibes of the ancient temples together with an excellent teacher will open your consciousness to different unseen levels.

YOGA

Yoga classes on the peaceful rooftop witnessing the rising sun is the perfect start of the day. Stepping fully into your body will support you to absorb the outstanding beauty of the Nile.

MASSAGE

After visiting the ancient healing temples, your body will be very grateful to receive a relaxing massage to integrate all the high energies.

SPECIAL DIETS

Our european trained chefs can provide you with any kind of gluten free, vegetarian or vegan diet. Detox menus are also a perfect way to help your body, mind and soul to release old toxines and take in all the good energies of the Nile.

WELLNESS

Your health is important to us. We can organise morning gym, sunrise walks or early evening runs. Bike rides in the dessert is also a fun way to workout. Our energy healers can teach you some ancient healing techniques or spoil you with authentic healing sessions.
